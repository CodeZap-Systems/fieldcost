# ✅ KEY TERMS & CONDITIONS

## Billing & Payment
- **Frequency**: Monthly or annual (auto-renews)
- **Annual Payment**: 15-17% discount (equivalent to 10 months cost)
- **Invoice**: Issued on 1st of each billing period
- **Currency**: All amounts in ZAR
- **VAT**: Applied to once-off fees (not recurring subscriptions for now)
- **Payment Method**: Direct bank transfer, credit card (4% surcharge), EFT

## Minimum Revenue Requirements (Targets, Not Guarantees)

| Tier | Target MRR | Timeline | Notes |
|------|-----------|----------|-------|
| Tier 1 | R7,990 (10 customers) | 6-9 months | Conservative estimate |
| Tier 2 | R29,985 (15 customers) | 12-18 months | Growth phase |
| Tier 3 | R60,000+ (2-3 deals) | 18-24 months | Enterprise sales cycle |

**Disclaimer**: These are illustrative estimates only. Actual results depend on sales execution, market conditions, and customer adoption.

## Exit / Acquisition Clause

If the business is sold or transferred:
- **Valuation Method**: 30x average monthly revenue share (last 6 months)
- **Example**: If dev averaged R3,000/month in last 6 months, buyout = R90,000
- **Two Options**: 
  1. Each party is bought out at 30x multiple, OR
  2. Revenue share agreements survive and bind the new owner
- **Applies To**: All three parties (IP Owner, Developer, Infrastructure Partner)
