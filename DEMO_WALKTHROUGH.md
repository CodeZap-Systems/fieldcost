## ❓ Common Questions & Answers

**Q: How is data isolated between companies?**
A: Each company sees only its own data. The system validates company_id on every request (GDPR/POPIA compliant).

**Q: Can multiple people work on the same project?**
A: Yes! Multiple team members can collaborate in real-time with activity tracking.

**Q: How do we integrate with our accounting system?**
A: Tier 3 includes Sage and Xero integration. Custom integrations available.

**Q: What about mobile access?**
A: Full responsive design works on all devices - web, tablet, mobile.

**Q: How is my data protected?**
A: Enterprise-grade security with encryption, regular backups, and compliance certifications.
