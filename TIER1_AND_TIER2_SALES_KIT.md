## 🚀 QUICK-START SALES GUIDE

### Sales Sequence (For Client's Sales Team)

#### 1. Discovery Call (15 minutes)
**Questions to ask every prospect**:
- How many field workers do you have?
- What's your current biggest operational challenge?
- Are you using any project management tools now?
- How do you track time for billing?
- Do you manually create invoices or use accounting software?
- What's your revenue run-rate?

**Scoring**:
- < 20 employees OR < R50k/month revenue → Lead with Tier 1
- 20-100 employees OR R50k-500k/month → Can start Tier 1, upsell to Tier 2
- 100+ employees OR > R500k/month → Show Tier 2 first, consider Tier 3

#### 2. Solution Matching (Show right tier)

**If they say "We're disorganized"** → Tier 1
- Show: Projects, tasks, kanban, invoicing
- Pitch: "Get organized in Tier 1, scale with Tier 2 later"

**If they say "We're growing too fast"** → Tier 2
- Show: GPS tracking, offline sync, WIP tracking
- Pitch: "Scale your operations without hiring 3 new admin people"

**If they say "We use Sage/Xero"** → Tier 2
- Show: ERP integration, auto-sync invoices
- Pitch: "Stop double-entering data. Auto-sync with Sage."

**If they say "We work in remote areas"** → Tier 2
- Show: Offline sync
- Pitch: "Teams work offline, sync automatically when back online"

**If they say "Money is tight"** → Tier 1
- Show: R799/month, feature-rich
- Pitch: "Best value for the money. Upgrade when you're ready."

#### 3. Demo (Show proof)
- Use live demo at: https://fieldcost.vercel.app (Tier 1)
- Use staging demo for Tier 2 features (after fix deployed)
- Let them try adding a project/task
- Show invoicing (biggest "wow" moment)

#### 4. Close (Ask for money)

**For Tier 1**:
> "So you get all of this - projects, tasks, invoicing, time tracking - for R799 a month. That's less than two cappuccinos per day. Ready to get started?"

**For Tier 2**:
> "Tier 2 adds GPS tracking, offline sync, and Sage integration. Your teams are worth R1,999/month in productivity improvements. When can you start?"

#### 5. Onboarding (Drive adoption)
- Send login credentials
- 1-hour training call
- Help create first project
- Let them practice for 2 weeks
- Follow up: "Any questions? What are you loving most?"
