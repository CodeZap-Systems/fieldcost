## 🔍 PRICING ANALYSIS

### Current Model Strengths ✅

| Strength | Benefit |
|----------|---------|
| **Tier 1 is affordable** | R599/month accessible to SMEs |
| **Clear progression** | Natural upgrade path: T1 → T2 → T3 |
| **Project-based add-on (T2)** | Scales with customer growth |
| **80/15/5 split is fair** | IP owner incentivized, dev protected |
| **Developer minimum retainer** | Protects dev from early-stage losses |
| **Exit protection (30x)** | Clear value transfer terms |

### Current Model Concerns ⚠️

| Concern | Impact | Why It Matters |
|---------|--------|---|
| **Tier 1 @ R599 may be underpriced** | Low barrier to entry could mean commoditization | Need to verify market positioning |
| **Tier 2 pricing is complex** (base + per-project) | Customer confusion on costs | Might need simplification |
| **"~R25k-R60k/year" for Tier 3 is too vague** | Enterprise deals unpredictable | Needs band clarity |
| **Developer only gets R900 at Tier 1 minimum** | May be insufficient for early support | Current retainer kicks at R25k MRR |
| **No annual discount mentioned for T1/T2** | Leaves revenue on table | Could incentivize longer contracts |
| **Project limits not defined for T2** | Unclear at what point client needs T3 | Scalability boundary unclear |

## 🎯 PRICING OPTIMIZATION RECOMMENDATIONS

### OPTION A: Maintain Current Pricing (Conservative)
**Recommendation**: Keep as-is if market validation shows:
- ✅ Tier 1 R599 attractive to target market
- ✅ Revenue growth trajectory acceptable
- ✅ Customer acquisition cost < R8,000/customer

**Pros**:
- Low barrier to entry
- Easy to remember
- Competitive on cost

**Cons**:
- Lower perceived value
- May attract price-sensitive customers
- Revenue targets harder to hit

---

### OPTION B: Increase Tier 1 Pricing (Recommended)
**Proposal**: 
- R599 → **R799/month** (+33%)
- Or: R599 → **R899/month** (+50%)

**Justification**:
- Features are worth more (full project mgmt + invoicing + time tracking)
- Local competitors charge R1,200-2,000
- Tier 2 gap is R900 (R1,499-599) - could be R700 (R1,499-799)

**Impact at R799/month**:
```
Tier 1 @ 10 customers:     R7,990 MRR (was R6,000)
IP Owner (80%):            R6,392 (vs R4,800) = +R1,592/mo
Developer (15%):           R1,199 (vs R900) = better earlier
Infra Partner (5%):        R399 (vs R300) = +R99/mo
```

**Recommendation**: ✅ **Best option for perceived value vs accessibility**

---

### OPTION C: Restructure Tier 2 Pricing (Clarify Complexity)

**Current Issue**: "R1,499 + R299/project" is ambiguous
- What if customer has 10 projects? (R1,499 + R2,990 = R4,489/mo)
- Is there a project limit?
- When do they need Tier 3?

**Proposal A: Seat-based instead of project-based**
```
Tier 2 OLD: R1,499 + R299/project
Tier 2 NEW: R1,999/month (5-10 users, unlimited projects)
           R2,999/month (10-20 users, advanced features)
```

**Proposal B: Keep project-based but add clarity**
```
Tier 2 NEW: R1,499 + R299/project (up to 15 projects)
            For 16+ projects: Tier 3 consultation needed
```

**Proposal C: Tiered project model**
```
Tier 2 Tier A (≤5 projects):    R1,499/month
Tier 2 Tier B (6-10 projects):  R2,299/month  
Tier 2 Tier C (10+ projects):   R3,299/month
```

**Recommendation**: ✅ **Proposal A (seat-based)** - Simpler for sales, easier to scale

---

### OPTION D: Enterprise Tier 3 (Make Quoting Clearer)

**Current Issue**: "~R25k-R60k/year" is too vague for prospects

**Proposal: Published pricing bands**
```
Tier 3 Annual Pricing (quoted per engagement):
├─ Small Enterprise (50-100 employees):      R20,000-30,000/year
├─ Mid Enterprise (100-300 employees):       R35,000-60,000/year
├─ Large Enterprise (300+ employees):        R75,000-150,000/year
└─ Includes: Unlimited users, unlimited projects, dedicated support, 
             integration support, quarterly business reviews

Plus per-customization add-ons:
├─ Custom API integration:                   R5,000-15,000 (one-time)
├─ Custom report builder:                    R3,000-8,000 (one-time)
└─ Dedicated account manager:                R2,000/month
```

**Recommendation**: ✅ **Publish clear bands** - reduces friction in sales cycle

## ⚠️ RISKS & MITIGATION

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|-----------|
| **Customer churn** | Existing customers may leave | Use 30-60 day grandfather clause, emphasize value |
| **Slower new sales** | Higher price may reduce conversion | Improve value messaging, add more features to justify |
| **Market rejection** | Market may not accept new pricing | Test with pilot customers first, validate demand |
| **Competitor undercut** | Competitors price lower | Position on value/support, not just raw price |
| **Tier 2 simplification confusion** | Customers may need more options | Publish clear feature comparison matrix |

**Summary of Changes**:
1. ✅ **Tier 1**: R599 → **R799/month** (+33%, better perceived value)
