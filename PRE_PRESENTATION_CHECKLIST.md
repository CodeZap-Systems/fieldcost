**Q: "What if something breaks after launch?"**
A: "We'll be monitoring 24/7. Any issue will be fixed immediately. We also have automatic backups, so no data loss."

**Q: "Can we customize it?"**
A: "Yes, absolutely. Let's make a list of customizations you need. Many are simple configuration changes."

**Q: "What about mobile?"**
A: "Works great on mobile - responsive design. Your team can use phones in the field just fine."

**Q: "How much does it cost?"**
A: "[Have pricing ready to quote or discuss] And Tier 1 is just the starting point - you can upgrade later."

**Q: "What's the learning curve?"**
A: "Very shallow. Most teams are comfortable after 1-2 hours of training. The interface is intuitive."

**Q: "How do you handle data security?"**
A: "Bank-level encryption. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Authentication required. Role-based access control."

**Q: "What if we need to get our data out later?"**
A: "Easy. We provide data exports in standard formats (CSV, JSON, etc.). You always have access to your own data."
