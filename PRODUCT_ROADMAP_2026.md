# 🚀 CRITICAL SUCCESS FACTORS

### 1. **Onboarding Velocity**
**Why**: Users must reach "aha moment" in first 10 minutes or churn  
**Tactical**:
- 1-click demo project load
- Video walkthrough (60 sec max)
- "First invoice" template ready to go
- WhatsApp support hotline (live)

### 2. **Feature/Market Fit**
**Why**: Build what contractors actually need, not what we think they need  
**Tactical**:
- Weekly user interviews (Tier 1 & 2 customers)
- Feature request voting in-app
- Contractor advisory board (3-5 power users)
- Teardown competitor workflows monthly

### 3. **Sales Engine**
**Why**: Manual sales critical until product-led growth scales  
**Tactical**:
- 1:1 sales calls by market segment (Contractor, PM, Crew)
- "Flip" strategy: Use pilot success to get referrals
- Strategic partnerships: Accounting firms, contractor groups
- Content strategy: "How to reduce WIP time by 40%" case studies

### 4. **Enterprise Readiness**
**Why**: R100k+ deals (Tier 3) require trust & compliance  
**Tactical**:
- Security audit (SOC 2 roadmap by Month 6)
- Data residency (RSA servers only)
- Compliance playbook (POPIA, tax withholding, BEE)
- Legal agreements pre-drafted (T2/T3 NDAs, DPAs)

### 5. **Churn Prevention**
**Why**: Retaining existing customers is 7x cheaper than acquiring new ones  
**Tactical**:
- In-app health scoring (engagement dashboard)
- Proactive outreach at day 14, 30, 60 (low activity)
- Monthly success calls for Tier 2+
- Usage-based upsell triggers
