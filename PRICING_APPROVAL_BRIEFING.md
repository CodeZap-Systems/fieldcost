## 🎯 EXECUTIVE BRIEF (5 Minutes)

### What We're Proposing
Update FieldCost pricing to reflect true value, simplify complexity, and improve revenue sustainability.

### The Bottom Line
- **Tier 1**: R599 → **R799/month** (+33%, same features)
- **Tier 2**: "R1,499+R299/proj" → **R1,999/month flat** (simpler, better value for customers with 5+ projects)
- **Tier 3**: "~R25-60k/yr" → **published bands: R25k-R150k/yr** (transparent, reduces sales friction)
- **Revenue Impact**: +18-33% higher revenue for same customer volumes

### Expected Outcomes
✅ Better revenue sustainability  
✅ Improved customer value perception  
✅ Clearer market positioning  
✅ Simpler sales conversations  
✅ Reduced customer confusion (especially Tier 2)

## ⚡ RISK ASSESSMENT

### Risk 1: Customer Churn (Medium Risk)

**Probability**: Low-Medium (only existing customers affected)  
**Impact**: Revenue loss from competitors

**Mitigation**:
- ✅ Use 30-60 day grandfather clause
- ✅ Offer annual discounts (15-17% vs month-to-month)
- ✅ Emphasize value improvements (new features)
- ✅ Proactive communication before change
- ✅ Loyalty pricing options for long-term customers

**Expected Outcome**: <5% churn, most stay for value + stability

---

### Risk 2: Reduced New Customer Conversion (Medium Risk)

**Probability**: Low (feature set justifies increase)  
**Impact**: Slower growth in Tier 1

**Mitigation**:
- ✅ Improve value messaging (what you get for R799)
- ✅ Emphasize ROI vs manual processes
- ✅ Offer 14-day free trial (let them experience value)
- ✅ Publish success stories (customers using platform)
- ✅ Focus on SME segment (less price-sensitive than individuals)

**Expected Outcome**: Small conversion dip initially, recovers with proper messaging

---

### Risk 3: Market Rejection (Low Risk)

**Probability**: Low (pricing still competitive)  
**Impact**: Sales stall

**Rationale for Low Risk**:
- R799 still significantly cheaper than international SaaS (~US$40/month = R720)
- Local competitors charged R1,200-2,000
- Value-to-price is strong given feature richness
- Tier 2 simplification actually offers better deals

**Mitigation**: Validate with 2-3 existing prospects before launch

---

### Risk 4: Team Disagreement (LOW Risk)

**Probability**: Low (all parties benefit)  
**Impact**: Implementation delay

**Why All Parties Support**:
- IP Owner: +31-80% more revenue
- Developer: +33% revenue share, hits retainers faster
- Infra Partner: +33% to offset growth overhead

**Mitigation**: All parties sign approval before public announcement
