## 🎯 STRATEGIC PRIORITIES (The 5 Things That Matter Most)

### 1. **Onboarding is Do-or-Die**
- Target: <10 minutes to "aha moment" (first invoice created)
- Tactic: 1-click demo project + video walkthrough
- Why: 50% of free users churn before trying product

### 2. **Sales Playbook by Persona**
- Contractors: "Stop paying accountants for WIP tracking"
- Project Managers: "Track budget vs actual in real-time"
- Field Crews: "Offline app that syncs when you get signal"
- Why: Different buyers need different value props

### 3. **Retention > Acquisition (Until M3)**
- Focus Days 1-30: Get first 10 customers
- Focus Days 31-90: Keep them (70% retention)
- Why: $100 customer that stays 2 years = $2,400 LTV

### 4. **Mobile is Non-Optional by Month 2**
- Field crews won't use web at construction site
- Offline first: Work where there's no signal
- Timeline: MVP by Day 60-90
- Why: Tier 2 requires field data capture

### 5. **Enterprise Features are Revenue Maximizers**
- Tier 3 targets "multi-company holding structures"
- Features: Multi-org RBAC, audit logs, custom branding
- Timeline: MVP by Month 5, 2-3 deals by Month 6
- Why: R20k annual deal = 33 Tier 1 customers' annual revenue

## 💡 QUICK WIN OPPORTUNITIES (Easy Wins in First 30 Days)

1. **WhatsApp Integration** (2 days dev)
   - Contractors live on WhatsApp
   - Send task updates via chat
   - Result: Better engagement + lower support load

2. **Time Tracking Notifications** (3 days dev)
   - "Your crew has been on Task XYZ for 4 hours"
   - "Budget exceeded alert"
   - Result: Reduce invoice disputes

3. **First-Contractor-to-Referral Program** (Ops only)
   - "Refer 3 friends, get 3 months free"
   - Target: Tap existing contractor networks
   - Result: CAC reduced to R0

4. **Accounting Firm Partnerships** (Sales + Ops)
   - Approach: "Free license + 20% commission on referrals"
   - Target: 5-10 accounting firms recommending us
   - Result: Qualified B2B2C leads monthly
